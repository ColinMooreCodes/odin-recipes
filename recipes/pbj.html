Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A true classic. Both for lunch and in programming. Bread can be toasted for added crunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 slickes Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Arrange both slices of bread face up.
  2. Liberally coat the upturned face of one slice with peanut butter to taste. Repeat the process on the other slice with the preferred amoutn of jelly.
  3. Press the coated faces of the bread slices together. Slice in half and serve.
